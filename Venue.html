Villa Vittoria Congress Palace

The ICPC 2015 venue is Firenze Fiera. Villa Vittoria, which it is a few steps away from the Santa Maria Novella Railway Station and only 5 km from the Amerigo Vespucci Airport.

The second floor of Villa Vittoria will host all the tracks and, in particular, the main room offers 190 seats and it will be the venue of the technical research track, while the side room contains 45 seats and it will be the place of all the other tracks. More information can be found here.
